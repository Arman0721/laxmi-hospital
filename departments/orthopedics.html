Bone & Joint Center

Firozabad's Leading Center for Joint Replacement & Trauma Reconstruction

The Orthopedics department at Laxmi Hospital Firozabad represents a standard of clinical competence in joint replacement, complex trauma fracture surgeries, spine stabilization, and minimal access arthroscopy. Led by veteran surgeon Dr. Amit Gupta, our surgical metrics ensure early mobilization and complete pain-free recoveries.

We combine advanced modular surgical spaces, HEPA-filtered clean air flow systems, and an integrated post-operative physiotherapy facility to treat bone eroding arthritis, sports ligament tears, and complex highway high-impact fractures.

Core Clinical Treatments Offered

  • Total Knee & Hip Replacements: High-durability clinical implants, precise surgical angles, and custom physical rehabilitation timelines.
  • Complex Fracture Reconstruction: Minimally invasive locking plate systems, intramedullary nailing for long bones, and open reduction alignments.
  • Ligament & Sports Arthroscopy: Keyhole repair of ACL/PCL tears, meniscus tears, and shoulder labral instabilities.
  • Spinal Surgery Care: Treatment for herniated discs, spinal stenoses, sciatica, and corrective spinal fixations.

Orthopedics FAQs

What is the expected recovery time for total knee replacement surgery?

Under Dr. Amit Gupta's advanced surgical technique, patients are encouraged to stand with support on the second day post-op. Complete independence with stairs and walking without walkers is generally achieved within 4 to 6 weeks of structured physiotherapy.

Does your hospital accept corporate or private health insurance for knee implants?

Yes. Laxmi Hospital is empanelled under all major private and government health insurance TPA partners. We provide complete transparent cost breakdowns prior to joint operations to ensure cashless approvals without hidden out-of-pocket costs.
