Advanced Gynecological Diagnostics & Safe Deliveries

The Gynecology & Obstetrics department at Laxmi Hospital Firozabad is dedicated to providing standard clinical protection for women during all stages of reproductive health and maternity. Led by senior specialist Dr. Sneha Singh, we combine gentle compassionate care with clinical precision.

Our labor suites are equipped with continuous fetal cardiotocography (CTG) monitoring, in-house painless delivery options (epidural analgesia), and emergency Caesarean section capabilities backed by our critical care and neonatal units.

Core Clinical Treatments Offered

  • High-Risk Pregnancy Care: Continuous surveillance for pre-eclampsia, gestational diabetes control, placenta previa, and multiple gestations.
  • Painless Delivery Suites: Supervised epidural management to ensure a smooth, pain-free labor experience in high hygiene settings.
  • Laparoscopic Uterine Surgeries: Minimal access keyhole removals of uterine fibroids, ovarian cysts, ectopic pregnancy resolutions, and hysterectomies.
  • Infertility & Hormonal Diagnostics: Comprehensive screening for PCOS, follicular monitoring studies, and hormonal corrective therapies.

Gynecology FAQs

What is the difference between normal and high-risk pregnancy?

A pregnancy is classified as high-risk if there are existing maternal conditions (like high BP, chronic diabetes, thyroid disorders, or maternal age over 35) or clinical indicators like twins or placenta previa. These cases require close monitoring and are handled under Dr. Sneha Singh's active care plans.

Do you have critical care units ready for obstetric emergency cases?

Yes. Laxmi Hospital has integrated maternal ICU beds and a level-3 Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) next to the delivery suites. This ensures that in any emergency, both mother and newborn receive immediate stabilization without transport delays.
