Comprehensive Neurological Therapeutics & Stroke Rehabilitations

The Neurology division at Laxmi Hospital Firozabad is a specialized therapeutic hub dedicated to treating systemic brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerve pathologies. We focus on reversing cerebral strokes, managing clinical epilepsy, stabilizing Parkinson's tremors, and resolving chronic neuropathic pain profiles.

Our team works closely with our high-resolution CT Scan lab and post-operative physical therapists to form integrated neurological recovery plans that optimize cognitive and physical motor functions.

  • Cerebral Stroke Management: Emergency clinical interventions, dynamic thrombolysis procedures, and stroke ICU stabilization.

  • Headache & Migraine Clinics: Targeted therapeutic blocking, trigger mapping, and chronic migraine preventive therapies.

Neurology FAQs

What is the "Golden Hour" in cerebral stroke events?

The Golden Hour refers to the first 4.5 hours from the onset of stroke symptoms (sudden facial drooping, arm weakness, or slurred speech). Reaching Laxmi Hospital's triage within this timeframe allows our team to inject clot-dissolving (thrombolytic) agents, which can prevent permanent brain paralysis.

Do you offer nerve conduction studies?

Yes. Laxmi Hospital coordinates with top-tier allied diagnostic centers to facilitate electroencephalography (EEG) and electromyography (EMG) profiles for diagnosing neurological disorders.
